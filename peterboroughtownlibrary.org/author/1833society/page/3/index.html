Peterborough Town Library

The Peterborough Town Library was established in 1833 as the first free taxpayer supported library in the United States. The 1833 Society is a nonprofit organization whose mission is to raise funds through private donations and grants to finance a major upgrade of the Peterborough Town Library and to assist in its development.
