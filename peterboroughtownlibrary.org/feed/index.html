Here are a few we love:

Written In My Heart’s Own Blood by Diane Gabaldon-available in audio and regular print.  From the Booklist Starred Review, “With her Outlander series, Gabaldon . . . successfully [juggles] a sizable and captivating cast of characters; developing thrilling plotlines that borrow equally from adventure, history, and romance; and meticulously integrating a wealth of fascinating period details into the story without slowing down the pace.”

The VB6 Cookbook: More Than 350 Recipes for Healthy Vegan Meals All Day and Delicious Flexitarian Dinners at Night by Mark Bittman

Following up on his bestselling diet plan, VB6, the incomparable Mark Bittman delivers a full cookbook of recipes designed to help you eat vegan every day before 6:00 p.m.–and deliciously all of the time.

The Supernatural Enhancements by Edgar Cantero

A mesmerizing novel…what begins as a gothic ghost story soon evolves into a wickedly twisted treasure hunt. Told vividly through a series of journal entries, cryptic ciphers, recovered security footage, and letters to a distant Aunt Liza, Edgar Cantero has written an absorbing, kinetic and highly original supernatural adventure with classic horror elements that introduces readers to a deviously sly and powerful new voice
Beyond the First Draft: The Art of Fiction

 

Beyond the First Draft by John Casey

National Book Award winner John Casey is a masterful novelist who is also an inspiring and beloved teacher. In Beyond the First Draft he offers essential and original insights into the art of writing—and rewriting—fiction.

Everything half price during July at the Friends of the Library’s Used Book Shop!

The Kyes-Sage Book Shop is hosting its Summer Book Sale during the entire month of July. Everything is half off already low prices, and sales benefit Peterborough Library programs and services.

Come browse our wide assortment of gently used books, CDs, audiobooks, DVDs, and other treasures. Stop in! We’re right next door to the Peterborough Library.

  • Thursdays 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
  • Fridays 10:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m.
  • Saturdays 10:00 a.m. to 1:00 p.m

We continue to replenish our stock, with new items added regularly throughout the summer.

The Friends of the Library donate all proceeds from this enterprise to Library programming and services. We thank you — and them — for this support!
