Join us for small community meetings at the Library during March and April.

By Peterborough Town Library on February 19, 2013 • ( Leave a comment )

If you didn’t get a chance to attend the Library Forum on February 16th,  you have other opportunities to add your voice as we imagine the Library of the Future. We’re hosting small, informal community meetings in March and April, downstairs at the Peterborough Town Library. Topics include Young Children, Children, Adult Readers & Services, Older Students & Adults, and Homeschoolers. Choose times, days and topics that interest you.

Add your voice in planning our Library of the Future

  • Sat, March 9th – 10-11 AM:
    Focus on Children
  • Thu, March 14th – 7-8 PM:
    Focus on Adult Readers & Services
  • Wed, March 20th – 10-11 AM:
    Focus on Young Children
  • Sat, March 23rd – 10-11 AM:
    Focus on Older Students & Adults
  • Wed, April 3rd – 10-11 AM: 
    Focus on Homeschoolers
  • Thu, April 4th – 7-8 PM:
    Focus on Adult Readers & Services

Your opinion and ideas are important. Choose the times, days and topics that interest you and join us. Topics are general and anyone interested should come. No RSVPs required.
